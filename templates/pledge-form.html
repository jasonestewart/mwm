Making Your Pledge

{$pledge_text}

When making a pledge we ask you to consider the total amount of money that you have described as income for the current year and to decide of that sum what is the maximum amount that you want to use for consumption and what is the maximum amount that you want to set aside for your future use (savings). That will leave you with an estimated amount that will be given away. We then ask you to decide to what charity you wish to donate that sum.
